Free parking is available in the parking garage located directly behind the clinic building. Please bring your parking ticket to the front desk so we can validate it for up to 2 hours.

## Dental Treatment Pricing
Here are our standard pricing details for common procedures:
- Routine Dental Cleaning & Exam: $120 (includes scaling, polishing, and standard X-rays).
- Teeth Whitening: $350 (professional in-office treatment).
- Composite (Tooth-Colored) Fillings: $150 to $250 depending on the size and location of the cavity.
- Emergency Dental Exam: $90 (covers diagnostics, consultation, and digital X-rays to assess sudden pain).
